# Vehicle Mods

Mods in this category modify vehicle mechanics in Borderlands 3.

These can include:

* Boost mechanics
* Vehicle health and damage
* Handling and physics
* Fuel or resource systems

## Cheat Mods

Mods designed to remove gameplay limitations or significantly enhance vehicle performance.

---

### Unlimited Vehicle Boost

File:

```
Cheat/Unlimited_Vehicle_Boost.bl3hotfix
```

Description:

Removes all vehicle boost drain systems to provide **true infinite boost**.

Features:

* No boost fuel consumption
* No boost activation cost
* Instant regeneration
* Works across all vehicles

Affected vehicles:

* Outrunner
* Technical
* Cyclone

---

### Unlimited Vehicle Health

File:

```
Cheat/Unlimited_Vehicle_Health.bl3hotfix
```

Description:

Prevents vehicles from being destroyed by drastically increasing health and disabling damage multipliers.

Features:

* Extremely large health pool
* Instant regeneration
* Damage multipliers removed

Vehicles become effectively **indestructible**.

Affected vehicles:

* Outrunner
* Technical
* Cyclone
